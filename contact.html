Common Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 3-5 working days depending on the type of work needed. Diamond cut repairs may take slightly longer. We'll give you a realistic timescale when you book.

Can you repair all types of alloy wheels?

We work on all makes and models of alloy wheels including diamond cut, powder coated, and painted finishes. If a wheel is structurally damaged beyond safe repair, we'll be honest and let you know.

Do I need to remove the wheels from my car?

Yes, wheels need to be removed for proper refurbishment. You can bring them to us already removed, or we can advise on how to do this safely.

What's the best way to send photos of damage?

WhatsApp is easiest - just send us clear photos of each damaged area plus a full wheel photo. Make sure the damage is in focus and well-lit. The more detail we can see, the more accurate our quote will be.

Do you offer mobile service?

We're workshop-based in Reading as we need specialist equipment for quality repairs. We serve Reading and the surrounding Berkshire area. Contact us to discuss collection and delivery options for an additional fee.
