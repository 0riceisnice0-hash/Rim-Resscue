Our Services

Expert alloy wheel refurbishment and repair for all vehicles across the UK

Alloy Wheel Refurbishment

Complete restoration of your alloy wheels to factory-fresh condition. Our comprehensive refurbishment process removes all signs of wear, damage, and deterioration, bringing your wheels back to life.

What This Fixes:

  • General wear and tear from daily driving
  • Surface scratches and scuff marks
  • Faded or discoloured finishes
  • Minor impact damage and imperfections
  • Oxidation and weathering

Perfect For:

All vehicle types including cars, vans, SUVs, and 4x4s. Ideal if you're preparing a vehicle for sale, want to refresh tired-looking wheels, or simply take pride in your motor. Whether you drive a family hatchback, luxury saloon, or performance car, professional refurbishment makes a huge difference.

The Process:

We strip back the old finish, repair any damage, prepare the surface meticulously, and apply a high-quality coating system. Each wheel is inspected at every stage to ensure the finish meets our exacting standards. The result is wheels that look brand new.

Diamond Cut Alloy Repair

Specialist diamond cutting service for precision-machined alloy wheels. Using professional CNC lathes, we recreate that mirror-finish shine that makes premium wheels stand out.

What This Fixes:

  • Corrosion on diamond cut faces
  • Lacquer peel and flaking
  • Kerb damage on machined surfaces
  • Oxidation and clouding
  • Loss of original mirror finish

Perfect For:

High-end vehicles with diamond cut finish wheels including Audi, BMW, Mercedes, Volkswagen, and other premium manufacturers. If your wheels have that distinctive machined face with a clear lacquer coat, this is the service you need. Common on modern alloys from 2010 onwards.

Why It Matters:

Diamond cut wheels can't simply be painted over. They require specialist machinery to precision-cut the face of the wheel, recreating the original finish. We use the same process as the factory, ensuring your wheels look exactly as they should. The finish is then protected with high-quality lacquer for long-lasting durability.

Kerb Damage Repair

Expert repair of kerb marks, scrapes, and impact damage. We've all done it - caught a kerb while parking or misjudged a tight corner. The good news is that most kerb damage is completely repairable.

What This Fixes:

  • Scuffs and scrapes from kerb contact
  • Gouges and chunks taken out of the rim
  • Paint damage from impacts
  • Bent or distorted wheel edges
  • Multiple kerb marks built up over time

Perfect For:

Anyone who's had that sinking feeling after clipping a kerb. Common on all vehicle types, especially in tight urban parking situations. Whether it's one wheel or all four, we'll have them looking perfect again. Even severe kerb damage that looks irreparable can usually be fixed by skilled hands.

The Fix:

We carefully rebuild the damaged area using specialist fillers and techniques, then blend the repair seamlessly into the original finish. The wheel is then refinished to match perfectly. You won't be able to tell where the damage was. Most repairs are completed quickly, getting you back on the road with wheels you can be proud of.

Wheel Painting and Colour Changes

Transform your wheels with custom colours and finishes. Whether you want to match a specific colour, go for a bold new look, or restore wheels to their original shade, we can make it happen.

What We Offer:

  • Full colour changes to any shade
  • Powder coating for extreme durability
  • Gloss, satin, or matte finishes
  • Shadow chrome and special effects
  • Two-tone and custom designs

Perfect For:

Car enthusiasts wanting to personalise their ride, anyone looking to refresh the look of their vehicle, or matching new wheels to existing ones. Popular with performance car owners, modified vehicles, and anyone who wants their wheels to stand out or blend perfectly with a new paint job.

Popular Choices:

Gloss black remains the most requested colour, giving a sleek, modern look. Anthracite grey, gunmetal, and silver are also very popular. We can match virtually any manufacturer colour code, or you can choose something completely unique. Powder coating offers the toughest finish available, perfect for wheels that face harsh conditions.

Corrosion and Lacquer Peel Repair

Professional repair of corroded wheels and peeling lacquer. UK weather can be brutal on alloy wheels, especially on diamond cut finishes. Salt, moisture, and temperature changes cause lacquer to break down and corrosion to take hold.

What This Fixes:

  • White corrosion spots and patches
  • Peeling and bubbling lacquer
  • Pitting and surface corrosion
  • Water ingress damage
  • Failed protective coatings

Perfect For:

Wheels that have been exposed to harsh winter conditions, coastal environments, or have simply aged. Very common on diamond cut wheels from premium German manufacturers. If left untreated, corrosion spreads rapidly and can make wheels unsafe. The earlier you catch it, the better.

Our Solution:

We completely strip the old, failed lacquer and address all corrosion at source. The wheel is prepared properly and refinished with high-quality, durable coatings. This isn't a quick fix that will fail again in six months - we do the job right. The wheels are treated to prevent future corrosion and sealed with professional-grade lacquer that can withstand UK weather conditions.
